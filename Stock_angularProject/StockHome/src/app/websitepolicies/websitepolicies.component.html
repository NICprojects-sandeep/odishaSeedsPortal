Website Policies

This is the Official website of Directorate of Agriculture and food production odisha, designed, developed and hosted by NIC.

The site has been developed to provide information to the general public. An attempt has been made through this site to provide reliable, comprehensive, accurate information about Seed and its various Entities. Hyperlinks at various places have been provided to other Government Portals / Websites.

The content in this site is the result of a collaborative effort of various Groups and Divisions of the Directorate. It is our endeavour to continue the enhancement and enrichment of this site in terms of content coverage, design and technology on a regular basis.
