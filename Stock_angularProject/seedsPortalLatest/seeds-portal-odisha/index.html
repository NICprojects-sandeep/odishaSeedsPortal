ABOUT SEED PORTAL DIRECTORATE

The Agriculture Department mainly consists of four executive wings namely, Directorate of Agriculture, Directorate of Horticulture, Directorate of Soil Conservation and Watershed Development Mission. Among all the agricultural inputs, seed is the most important basic and critical input required for sustainable agriculture. The responses of all other inputs depend on quality seeds to a large extent.
